## What Is an AI Phone Agent?

An AI phone agent is software that answers your business phone using natural-sounding voice AI. It's not a clunky IVR menu ("press 1 for sales"). It's a conversational agent that can:

- Answer questions about your services, hours, and pricing
- Book appointments directly into your calendar
- Capture caller details and send them to your CRM or inbox
- Transfer urgent calls to you or your team
- Handle multiple calls simultaneously

Think of it as a receptionist that never takes a break, never calls in sick, and costs a fraction of a salary.

## How the Technology Works (Simply)

An AI phone agent for small business uses three layers of technology:

### 1. Speech Recognition

The agent converts the caller's voice into text in real time. Modern systems handle accents, background noise, and natural speech patterns with over 95% accuracy.

### 2. Language Understanding

A large language model (like the ones behind ChatGPT) processes what the caller said and decides the best response. It's been trained on your business info – your services, your FAQs, your booking rules.

### 3. Voice Synthesis

The response is converted back to natural-sounding speech. Today's voice AI sounds remarkably human – most callers can't tell the difference.

The whole cycle happens in under a second. The caller experiences a normal phone conversation.

## Is an AI Phone Agent Right for Your Business?

An AI phone agent for small business makes sense if:

- You're missing more than 10% of incoming calls
- You or your staff spend significant time on routine phone queries
- You get calls outside business hours that go to voicemail
- Each new customer is worth £200 or more to your business

If any of those apply, the ROI is typically clear within the first month.
